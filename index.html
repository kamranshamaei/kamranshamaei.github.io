ABOUT ME

I am a roboticist. I have extensive industrial experience as a robotics software, systems, and hardware engineer as well as scientist.

To summarize my software skills, I can and do implement robotic algorithms from mathematical concepts, Matlab scripts, Python prototype scripts, all the way to C++ production software. I am well familiar with common realtime and non-realtime platforms such as QNX and Linux. I also have hands-on experience working on embedded systems such as ARM and Freescale processors...

To elaborate on my robotics knowledge, I have extensive hands-on experience developing algorithms for forward and inverse kinematics, manipulator calibration, kinematics redundancy and nullspace, trajectory generation and collision avoidance. I also have worked and implemented algorithms and systems that involved machine learning...

To explain my systems and hardware skills, I have been involved in the complete concept development, requirement implementation, development, verification and validation, clinical experimentation and regulatory processes for surgical and medical robots. I have also designed and licensed purely mechanical devices as well as electromechnical systems. I also have controls experience such as multilateral teleoperated systems, admittance control, cascaded PID controllers and stability and transparency analysis...

My education has been in Robotics from a Mechanical Engineering side, which in combination with my industrial experience as a software engineer, has given me the ability to understand and analyze complex robots at a systems level. I enjoy being involved in development of robotic systems throughout their development cycle.
